🔌 Magnetic Force on a Wire

F = B · I · L · sin θ

⚡ Magnetic Force on a Charge

F = q · v · B · sin θ

📌 Key Concepts

  • The angle θ is measured between the direction of current (or velocity) and the magnetic field B.
  • When θ = 90°, sin θ = 1, giving the maximum force.
  • When θ = 0° or 180°, sin θ = 0, so the force is zero.
  • The direction of the force is given by the right-hand rule (not calculated here).
  • 1 Tesla (T) = 1 kg/(A·s²) = 1 N/(A·m)
