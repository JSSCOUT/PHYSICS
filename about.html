About ElectroMag Calculator

ElectroMag Calculator is an interactive web application designed to help students understand and solve Electricity and Magnetism problems. Whether you're working through Ohm's Law, calculating electrostatic forces with Coulomb's Law, or finding magnetic forces on wires and charges, this tool provides instant, accurate results with clear explanations.

The project was built as an educational resource — combining interactive calculators, a comprehensive formula reference, and fully worked sample problems to support students at every stage of their E&M studies. No sign-up required, no external libraries — just pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

We believe that learning physics should be accessible, intuitive, and even enjoyable. ElectroMag Calculator is our contribution to making that a reality.
