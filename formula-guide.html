📐 Formula Guide

A complete reference for all Electricity and Magnetism formulas, symbols, and units.

All Formulas

Use this table as a quick reference during problem solving.

Topic Formula Symbols Units
Electric Charge Q = ne Q = total charge, n = number of electrons, e = 1.6×10⁻¹⁹ C (elementary charge) Coulombs (C)
Coulomb's Law F = kq₁q₂ / r² k = 9.0×10⁹ N·m²/C², q₁ & q₂ = point charges, r = distance between charges Newtons (N)
Electric Field E = F/q  or  E = kQ/r² E = electric field strength, F = force on test charge, q = test charge, Q = source charge, r = distance N/C (Newtons per Coulomb)
Ohm's Law V = IR V = voltage (potential difference), I = current, R = resistance Volts (V)
Electric Power P = VI P = power, V = voltage, I = current Watts (W)
Resistance R = V / I R = resistance, V = voltage, I = current Ohms (Ω)
Magnetic Force (Wire) F = BIL sin θ B = magnetic field, I = current, L = length of wire in field, θ = angle between wire and B Newtons (N)
Magnetic Force (Charge) F = qvB sin θ q = charge, v = velocity of charge, B = magnetic field, θ = angle between v and B Newtons (N)
